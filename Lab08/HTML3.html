Dwayne Johnson's Profile

Biography:

Also known as "The Rock," Dwayne spends his days fighting crime and just being an all around great guy. Dwayne has saved the world one too many times. To name a few of his great moments... he saved us all from a giant gorilla, journeyed to the center of the earth, traveled to witch mountain, fought off a hurricane, and is a toothfairy and lifeguard in his spare time.

If you see a man in a jungle it's probably Dwayne...
